Forge Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Overview

Forge is a task management and productivity application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Forge does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All your tasks, projects, notes, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs of any kind.

Data Storage

All data created within Forge is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This includes tasks, projects, reminders, focus timer history, and app preferences. Your data never leaves your device.

Voice Input

Forge offers optional voice dictation for creating tasks. Speech recognition is performed entirely on-device using Android's built-in speech recognizer. No audio data is transmitted to external servers by Forge.

Network Access

Forge does not require an internet connection to function. The app may access the network solely for Google Play Billing if you choose to purchase Forge Pro. No user data is transmitted during this process.

Third-Party Services

Forge does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The only third-party service used is Google Play Billing for in-app purchases, which is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Forge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

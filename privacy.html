Overview

Fuel Finder UK - Live Prices ("the App", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, how it is used, and who it is shared with. The App does not require an account, sign-up, or login.

Most of your information — your saved vehicles, favourites, preferences, and searches — stays on your device. Some features, described below, require data to be sent to UK government services or to our own and third-party servers in order to work. This policy explains exactly when that happens.

Information We Handle

1. Location Data

  • What: Approximate and precise location from your device, and any start/destination you enter for route planning.
  • Why: To show fuel stations near you, sort them by distance, and (in Plan Route) calculate a driving route and find the cheapest fuel along it.
  • Where it goes: Sorting stations by distance happens on your device. When you use Plan Route, the start and destination coordinates are sent to our server to calculate the route. Map tiles and ads (see below) may allow third parties to infer your approximate location from your IP address.
  • Storage: We do not store your location history on our servers or build a profile of your movements.

2. Vehicle Information (My Cars)

  • What: The vehicle registration number you choose to enter, and the details returned for it (make, model, fuel type, MOT history, tax and ULEZ status).
  • Why: To show your vehicle's MOT history, tax and ULEZ status, road tax rate, and to calculate fill costs based on tank size.
  • Where it goes: When you add or refresh a vehicle, the registration number is sent to the DVLA Vehicle Enquiry Service and the DVSA MOT History service (both UK government services) to retrieve the vehicle's details. We do not store your registration number or vehicle details on our own servers.
  • Storage: Returned vehicle details are stored locally on your device and are deleted when you remove the vehicle or uninstall the App.

3. Community Reports

  • What: Reports you choose to submit (for example: out-of-fuel, queue times, closed station, suspected price errors), the station the report relates to, the report time, and your votes on other reports.
  • Why: To show real-time station status to other drivers and let the community verify reports.
  • Where it goes: Reports and votes are sent to and stored in our cloud database, hosted on Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore, and are visible to other users of the App. Reports are not linked to a name or account (the App has no accounts). A non-identifying random identifier may be used to prevent duplicate voting.

4. User Preferences

  • What: Selected fuel type and grade, preferred brands, search radius, sort order, theme and accent colour, and favourite stations.
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only and never sent to our servers.

5. Advertising & Identifiers

  • What: If you consent, your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and ad-interaction data may be used to show personalised ads. If you do not consent, ads are non-personalised.
  • How we ask: We use Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt and, in the UK/EU, a Google-certified consent (UMP) prompt before any tracking or personalised advertising.
  • Provider: Google AdMob (see Third-Party Services).

6. Device & Diagnostic Information

  • What: Basic technical information (such as iOS version and device type) and your IP address, which is necessarily visible to any server the App contacts.
  • Why: To deliver content, ensure compatibility, and keep the service secure and reliable.

How We Use Your Information

  1. Find and rank nearby fuel stations based on your location.
  2. Display fuel prices sourced from official UK government (CMA) open data.
  3. Plan routes and find the cheapest fuel along your journey.
  4. Show your vehicle's MOT, tax and ULEZ information in My Cars.
  5. Power Community Reports so drivers can share live station status.
  6. Remember your preferences and improve app performance.
  7. Show advertising (unless you have purchased the ad-free upgrade).

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your personal information.
  • ❌ Create a profile of your movements or location history.
  • ❌ Store your vehicle registration number on our own servers.
  • ❌ Require an account, name, or email address to use the App.

Data Retention

  • On-device data (preferences, favourites, saved vehicles and their details) stays on your device and is deleted when you remove it in the App or uninstall.
  • Community Reports you submit are retained in our database and may remain visible to others until they expire or are removed. Because they are not linked to an account, contact us if you need a specific report removed.
  • Vehicle registration numbers are sent to DVLA/DVSA only to perform a lookup and are not stored on our servers.

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions

You can revoke location access at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Fuel Finder UK. You can still search by entering a location manually if you deny access.

Tracking & Ad Personalisation

You can change tracking permission via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and opt out of Apple's personalised ads via Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising. Purchasing the one-time ad-free upgrade removes all ads.

Data Deletion

  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all data stored on your device.
  • To request removal of a Community Report, email support@fuelfinderuk.live.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. It contains no age-inappropriate content.

Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR / EU GDPR)

For users in the UK and EU, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: Location access, App Tracking Transparency, and personalised advertising.
  • Legitimate Interest: Providing nearby station results, Community Reports, security, and app reliability.
  • Performance of a service you request: Vehicle (DVLA/DVSA) lookups and route calculation, which run only when you ask for them.

Your GDPR Rights

If you are in the UK or EU, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing of your personal data, and to data portability. Because the App holds most data only on your device and uses no accounts, many of these are exercised directly on your device. For anything else, contact support@fuelfinderuk.live. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Data Sources

  • UK Government Open Data Scheme: Fuel prices published by UK retailers under the Motor Fuel Price (Open Data) Regulations 2025, administered by the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA). Updated approximately every 30 minutes.

Privacy Policy for Fuel Finder UK - Live Prices

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Developer: DF Labs

Contact: support@fuelfinderuk.live

Version: 2

Overview

Fuel Finder UK - Live Prices ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

  • What We Collect: Approximate and precise location data from your device
  • Why We Collect It: To show you fuel prices at petrol stations near your current location
  • How It's Used: Location data is used only to calculate distances to fuel stations and sort results by proximity
  • Storage: Location data is NOT stored on our servers. It is processed locally on your device only.

2. User Preferences

  • What We Collect: Your selected fuel type (petrol/diesel/LPG), preferred brands, search radius, and sort preferences
  • Why We Collect It: To remember your preferences and provide a personalized experience
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted to external servers.

3. Device Information

  • What We Collect: Basic device information (iOS version, screen size) for app compatibility
  • Why We Collect It: To ensure the app works correctly on your device
  • Storage: Used only during app operation, not stored permanently

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Find nearby fuel stations based on your location
  2. Display fuel prices from official UK government data sources
  3. Personalize your experience by remembering your fuel type and brand preferences
  4. Improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
  • ❌ Share your location data with advertisers
  • ❌ Store your location on external servers
  • ❌ Track your movements or create location history

Third-Party Services:

Google Maps Platform

  • Used for: Displaying maps and providing navigation to fuel stations
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • What they collect: Location data when you use maps or navigation features

Google AdMob (for advertisements)

  • Used for: Displaying banner advertisements
  • Privacy Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
  • What they collect: Advertising ID, device information, and interactions with ads

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All fuel price data comes from official UK government open data sources
  • Location data is processed locally on your device
  • User preferences are stored securely using iOS's encrypted storage
  • We do not transmit your personal information to external servers

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions

You can revoke location access at any time through your device Settings → Privacy → Location Services → Fuel Finder UK - Live Prices

If you deny location access, you can still search for fuel prices by entering a location manually.

Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising

Data Deletion

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your preferences and data
  • We do not retain any data after uninstallation

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is intended for general audiences and does not contain age-inappropriate content.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Data Sources

This app uses fuel price data from:

  • UK Government Open Data Scheme - Official fuel prices published by UK retailers under the CMA (Competition and Markets Authority) open data requirements
  • Data is publicly available and updated every 30 minutes

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the UK and EU, we process your data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide you with nearby fuel station information
  • Consent: Location access requires your explicit permission
  • Contract Performance: To deliver the app's core functionality

Your GDPR Rights

If you are located in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@fuelfinderuk.live

Why the CMA Data Matters

Official government data, made easy to use — here's how it works.

🏛️

What is the CMA?

The Competition and Markets Authority is the UK government body responsible for ensuring fair markets and protecting consumers. They introduced mandatory fuel price reporting to bring greater transparency to forecourt pricing across the UK.

⚖️

Why does this matter for fuel prices?

The CMA Fuel Finder scheme makes forecourt pricing publicly available for the first time. With real pricing data at your fingertips, you can easily compare what stations near you are charging and choose the one that offers the best value.

📱

How does this app help?

Fuel Finder UK uses official CMA data that all major retailers are legally required to report. With live prices from thousands of stations in one place, you can quickly compare and choose the best deal near you — all for free, with no account needed.

The bottom line: Having access to accurate, up-to-date fuel prices helps you make better decisions at the pump. This app puts that information in your hands — for free, with no sign-up, using the same official data published by the UK Government.

Where Does Fuel Finder UK Get Its Data?

Fuel Finder UK is a free app that compares live petrol and diesel prices at over 6,000 UK stations. It uses the official UK Government CMA dataset — updated every 30 minutes — so you always see real, verified prices from retailers like Tesco, Asda, BP, and Shell. No sign-up needed, available on Android and iOS.

Why is my local station missing?

Fuel Finder UK only shows stations that appear in the official UK Government CMA fuel price dataset. Retailers with 100+ sites are legally required to submit prices. Smaller retailers can voluntarily join. If your station isn't listed, you can encourage them to register with the CMA scheme or report it to the CMA. Coverage grows as more retailers join — currently 6,000+ stations and rising.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my local station missing?
Fuel Finder UK only shows stations in the official UK Government CMA fuel price dataset. Retailers with 100+ sites must submit prices by law. Smaller ones can join voluntarily. Coverage is currently 6,000+ stations and growing.
What's the cheapest petrol near me?
Download Fuel Finder UK on Android or iOS and the app will instantly show you the cheapest petrol and diesel stations near your location, ranked by price and distance — updated every 30 minutes from official CMA data.
How do I find cheap diesel near me?
Open the app, select Diesel as your fuel type, and you'll see the cheapest diesel stations near you sorted by price. You can also filter by brand and plan a route to find the cheapest diesel along any journey.
Which supermarket has the cheapest fuel?
Tesco, Asda, Sainsbury's, and Morrisons fuel prices are all included. Use the brand filter to compare supermarket fuel prices side by side, updated every 30 minutes from the official CMA dataset.
Is there a free petrol price app for the UK?
Yes! Fuel Finder UK is completely free on both Android and iOS. There's no subscription, no account needed, and no paywall. Just download and start comparing prices.
Does it work on iPhone / iOS?
Yes! Fuel Finder UK is available on both Android (Google Play) and iOS (App Store). Download it free on either platform — no sign-up needed.
What is Community Reporting?
Community Reporting lets drivers report real-time issues at fuel stations — like being out of petrol, queue times, or other problems. Reports are voted on by the community and shown to all users. Available on both Android and iOS.
How accurate are the fuel prices?
Very accurate. Prices come from the official UK Government CMA dataset, which all major fuel retailers are legally required to update. Prices refresh every 30 minutes.
Can I find the cheapest fuel along a route?
Yes. Use the Plan Route feature — enter your start and destination, and the app shows you the cheapest fuel stations along your entire journey. Perfect for long motorway trips.
Does it support E10 and premium fuels?
Yes. Fuel Finder UK supports all fuel grades tracked by the CMA scheme: E10 petrol, premium petrol, standard diesel, premium diesel (B7P), diesel B10, and more.
Can I remove the ads?
Yes. A one-time in-app purchase removes all adverts permanently and unlocks Android Auto support. No subscription — just a single payment to support development.
What is the My Cars feature?
My Cars lets you track your vehicles inside the app. View MOT history and advisories, ULEZ status, tax reminders, fill cost calculations linked to your tank size, and road tax rates — everything to stay on top of car ownership.
Does Fuel Finder UK support Android Auto?
Yes! With the Premium upgrade, Fuel Finder UK works on Android Auto. Find the cheapest fuel nearby from your car's dashboard, sort by price or distance, filter by brand, and navigate directly to any station.
Where does the price data come from?
All prices come from the UK Government Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) open fuel price dataset. Retailers with 100+ filling stations are legally required to submit their prices. The data refreshes every 30 minutes from 14 official retailer feeds.
Who Built This?

Built by Darren Fernandez

Fuel Finder UK was created by Darren Fernandez, a UK-based indie developer, during the cost-of-living crisis. The goal was simple: take the power of official government fuel price data and make it accessible to everyone — right on their phone. What started as a side project to help drivers save money has grown to cover 6,000+ stations across the UK on both Android and iOS.

Fuel Finder UK - Live Prices (also known as Fuel Finder UK or Fuel Finder) is a free Android app that shows real-time petrol and diesel prices at more than 8,000 fuel stations across the United Kingdom. Prices are sourced from the UK Government's Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) open fuel price dataset, which all major retailers are legally required to update — and are refreshed in the app every 30 minutes. The app covers supermarket fuel stations including Tesco, Asda, Sainsbury's, and Morrisons, as well as branded forecourts such as BP, Shell, and Esso. It requires no account or sign-up and is completely free to download from Google Play.

Why the CMA Data Matters

🏛️

What is the CMA?

The Competition and Markets Authority is the UK government body responsible for ensuring fair markets and protecting consumers. They introduced mandatory fuel price reporting to bring greater transparency to forecourt pricing across the UK.

⚖️

Why does this matter for fuel prices?

The CMA Fuel Finder scheme makes forecourt pricing publicly available for the first time. With real pricing data at your fingertips, you can easily compare what stations near you are charging and choose the one that offers the best value.

📱

How does this app help?

Fuel Finder UK - Live Prices (also known simply as Fuel Finder or Fuel Finder UK) uses official CMA data that all major retailers are legally required to report. With live prices from thousands of stations in one place, you can quickly compare and choose the best deal near you — all for free, with no account needed.

The bottom line: Having access to accurate, up-to-date fuel prices helps you make better decisions at the pump. This app puts that information in your hands — for free, with no sign-up, using the same official data published by the UK Government.
